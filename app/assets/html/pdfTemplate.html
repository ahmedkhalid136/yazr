INVESTMENT THESIS
What we like
  • Unique product and differentiated positioning on its target market (large AMs), with a category leader potential in Europe
  • Strong commercial performance since Series A with flagship RFPs won (Generali, AXA IM)
  • Highly positive customer feedback, 0 churn
  • Clear shift upmarket since Series A, with several contracts in the €400-600k range
  • Well-balanced team with key Enterprise Sales talent
  • Positive traction in the UK market, with the milestone of signing Fidelity, a flagship UK client
Unknown and risks
  • Growth may plateau once all Tier 1 asset managers are equipped.
  • While Weefin can capture a significant market share, it will be within a mid-sized market, estimated at approximately €500 million for large, publicly listed asset managers.
  • While ESG reporting is expected to remain mandatory and a core priority for large asset managers, regulatory changes could impact the urgency of adopting a tool like Weefin.
Next steps
